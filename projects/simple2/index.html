Project Two

Project – SIMPLE (Sales Client Communication Simulation)

A branching, video‑driven choose‑your‑own‑adventure experience that builds confident client communicators through timely mentor feedback and targeted micro‑skills.

View Live Simulation

Role
Learning & Performance Designer, Prototyper
Focus
Conversation skills, objection handling, discovery
Toolkit
HTML/CSS/JS, Synthesia video, lightweight state engine
Outcome
Faster ramp, consistent messaging, measurable practice

The Challenge

Sales and client‑facing teams need safe practice to build confidence, but traditional courses rarely simulate the pressure and branching choices of a real conversation. Messaging drifts, skills vary by rep, and coaching time is limited.

Considerations

  • Practice must be fast, repeatable, and relevant to real calls.
  • Guidance should target micro‑skills: rapport, probing, summarizing, next steps.
  • Feedback works best when it is just‑in‑time and actionable.
  • Leaders need a repeatable framework to coach consistently.

The Solution

SIMPLE is a branching, video‑based simulation with a mentor side‑panel that reacts to choices in real time, reinforcing the behaviors that drive better client outcomes.

  • Branching video paths emulate realistic client reactions.
  • Mentor feedback panel delivers tips, hints, and rationale as you choose.
  • Score rubric maps choices to core skills (e.g., discovery, empathy, clarity).
  • Retry & reflection loops encourage deliberate practice.
  • Analytics‑ready events (choice, time‑to‑answer) for future dashboarding.

Reflection

  • Simulations like SIMPLE shift learning from knowing to doing—building confidence where it matters most: live with a client.
  • The mentor panel balances encouragement with specificity, making coaching scalable without losing the human voice.
  • Next step: plug in a lightweight rubric service so managers can tailor weighting by role or region.

Buildout

  • Produced scenario clips with Synthesia for speed and consistency.
  • Implemented a compact state engine (vanilla JS) to track choices and surface feedback.
  • Designed a clean, accessible UI with clear affordances and keyboard navigation.
  • Structured events for future analytics (CSV export or API hook).

Timeline

  • Day 1: Scenario mapping, scriptwriting, and rubric design
  • Day 2: Video production and UI shell
  • Day 3: Branching logic, mentor feedback, polish
