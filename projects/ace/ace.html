ACE AI Coach

A just-in-time learning assistant for Compliance process support

Live Demo Watch Demo

The Challenge

Working with Compliance to approve content should be straightforward, but it’s often not. Complex procedures, documentation requirements, and understanding who does what (and when) can be overwhelming, even for seasoned professionals...

The Insight

Instead of developing another static course, I started by asking the question: "What if the learning experience met users at their moment of need?"...

The Solution

ACE was born: An AI-powered digital coach designed to deliver efficient, contextualized guidance on the Compliance process—without requiring learners to wade through irrelevant content...

  • Delivers just-in-time support based on real user queries.
  • Reduces reliance on long-format courseware.
  • Improves efficiency and engagement by skipping what's irrelevant.
  • Creates a scalable, extensible framework for future AI learning tools.

The Reflection

ACE demonstrates how learning designers can leverage AI not just to build content, but to create support systems that evolve alongside the learner...

The Build

  • Designed a modern, bubble-style UI that feels familiar and inviting.
  • Developed an agent personality and knowledge base.
  • Connected to OpenAI’s GPT API via Node.js backend.
  • Styled the interface for clarity, accessibility, and professionalism.

The Timeline

  • Day 1: Ideation, layout wireframe, and conversation flow.
  • Day 2: Frontend development, styling, mascot integration.
  • Day 3: Backend integration, testing, and deployment.
