Project - ACE AI Coach

The Challenge

Working with Compliance to approve content should be straightforward, but it’s often not. Complex procedures, documentation requirements, and understanding who does what (and when) can be overwhelming, even for seasoned professionals.

  • A traditional, one-size-fits-all course could help, but it could feel irrelevant for experienced professionals and frustrating for newer ones looking for immediate answers.
  • A reference guide could help, but where does one find the right answer?
  • Support teams could help but field repeated questions about the process that a smarter solution could handle.

The Insight

Instead of developing another static course, I asked: “What if the experience met users at their moment of need?” With recent advances in AI, I realized I could prototype a conversational coach that adapts to the user’s level and questions.

The Solution

ACE was born: An AI-powered digital coach designed to deliver efficient, contextualized guidance on the Compliance process—without requiring learners to wade through irrelevant content. ACE (Assistant for Compliance Excellence): 

  • Delivers just-in-time support based on real user queries.
  • Reduces reliance on long‑format courseware.
  • Improves efficiency and engagement by skipping what's irrelevant.
  • Creates a scalable AI learning framework
