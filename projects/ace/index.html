ACE AI Coach

A smarter way to support compliance learning—right when it’s needed.

The Challenge

Working with Compliance to approve content should be straightforward, but it’s often not. Complex procedures, documentation requirements, and understanding who does what (and when) can be overwhelming, even for seasoned professionals.

The Insight

Instead of developing another static course, I started by asking: "What if the learning experience met users at their moment of need?"

The Solution

ACE is an AI-powered digital coach that delivers contextual guidance for the Compliance process—just-in-time and learner-centered.

  • Supports real-time decision making
  • Reduces reliance on long-format content
  • Improves accuracy and engagement
  • Builds a scalable framework for future tools
